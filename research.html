Current interests

  • Characteristics of artificial intelligence and machine learning methods in the context of cognitive science. Properties shared between artificial and biological systems.
  • Formal models of categorization, links between human category learning and data complexity.
  • Coordination and multimodal synchrony in collective cognitive systems (e.g. committees of experts).
Past research topics
  • Machine learning with emphasis on ensemble classifiers, classifiers diversity, data complexity and methods for building consensus from multiple predictions.
  • Applications of machine learning in bioinformatics. Facilitating reconstruction of protein interaction network by predicting not yet observed interactions based on the available experimental data.
