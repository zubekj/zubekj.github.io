About me

I value a good book, imaginative conversation, and Chinese tea drank from a sky blue bowl. During summer I like backpacking in some nice mountains, preferably forested.

Hobbies

Martial arts are among my long-lasting interests. I used to practice judo for many years. Now I study yiquan – a modern style of kung fu which emphasises spontaneity and the usage of imagination during training. You can visit the site of my school: Akademia Yiquan or Yiquan Academy.

I am interested in various personality typologies, most notably socionics, which is a post-jungian typology heavily influenced by Antoni Kępiński's concept of information metabolism. I participate in editing a Polish site about this system with an active forum.
