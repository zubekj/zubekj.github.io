I am a cognitive scientist with a keen interest in studying fundamental aspects of interpersonal interactions.

My formal background is in computer science, and my PhD thesis focused on applications of machine learning in bioinformatics. Currently, I work as an assistant professor at the Human Interactivity and Language Lab within the Faculty of Psychology at the University of Warsaw.

Here is my full CV.

Research interests

  • Embodied and enactive theories of cognition.
  • Interpersonal coordination and multimodal synchrony in interaction.
  • Agent-based models of language emergence.

You can find the list of my publications on Google Scholar
