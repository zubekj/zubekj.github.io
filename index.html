I am a programmer and data scientist with an unusual predilection for unapplied social sciences.

Currently I work at the Laboratory of Functional and Structural Genomics, CeNT UW. I also pursue PhD at the Institute of Computer Science, PAS.

Projects

During my master studies I co-developed Skarb – an experimental Ruby to C compiler with some interesting optimizations of memory management.

Now I am active in research concerning cognitive systems, machine learning and its applications in bioinformatics. You can find the list of my publications on Google Scholar.

Some by-products of my scientific activity can be found as code repositories on my Github account.
